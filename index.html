How It Works for Drivers

Turn your plow into a profit machine. Here's how you can start earning with Plowshare.

Sign Up & Get Verified

Create your driver profile, upload your license and insurance documents, and complete a quick background check. Get verified in as little as 24 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Find out everything you need to know about Plowshare.

Plowshare is launching in select markets across the Midwest and Northeast United States, where snow removal is a seasonal necessity. We're starting with major metropolitan areas and surrounding suburbs, then expanding based on demand.

Sign up for our waitlist to be notified when we launch in your area. If there's enough interest in your region, we'll prioritize expanding there!

Pricing varies based on your driveway size and service type. On-demand requests typically start at $35-75 for standard residential driveways, with prices displayed upfront before you confirm.

Subscription plans offer significant savings—typically 20-30% off on-demand rates. You'll get added to a driver's roster for automatic service after every snowfall above your chosen threshold (usually 2+ inches).

To join Plowshare as a driver, you'll need:

  • A valid driver's license and clean driving record
  • Your own snow plow equipment (truck with plow attachment, or commercial-grade equipment)
  • Proof of liability insurance
  • Pass a background check
  • A smartphone to use the Plowshare driver app

We verify all drivers before they can accept jobs on the platform to ensure quality and safety for our customers.

During major storm events, demand naturally increases. Here's how we handle it:

  • Subscription customers get priority: Drivers serve their roster customers first
  • Transparent wait times: We show estimated wait times upfront so you can plan accordingly
  • No surge pricing: Unlike some services, we don't increase prices during storms
  • Safety first: If conditions are too dangerous, we may temporarily pause service until roads are safe

For the best experience during heavy snow seasons, we recommend subscribing to a driver's roster for guaranteed service.

The Plowshare app will be available on both iOS and Android. We're currently in development and planning to launch before the next winter season.

Join our waitlist to:

  • Get notified the moment we launch in your area
  • Receive early access to try the app before the general public
  • Lock in special introductory pricing for early adopters

On-demand requests: You can cancel free of charge before a driver is assigned. Once a driver accepts your request, a small cancellation fee may apply.

Subscriptions: Cancel anytime with no penalties. Your service continues until the end of your current billing period. We believe in earning your business each month, not locking you into contracts.
